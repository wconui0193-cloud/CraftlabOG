# PROCESS PHILOSOPHY

Every project starts with a Process Map.

The Process Map acts as the blueprint for the entire customer journey.

Without a Process Map:

* Workflows become messy
* Automations become disconnected
* Systems become difficult to manage

The Process Map defines:

Lead enters

Lead nurtured

Discovery call booked

Proposal sent

Customer won

Customer lost

Re-engagement sequence

Everything follows a defined process.

---

# CUSTOMER JOURNEY

1. Lead submits form

2. Lead enters CRM

3. Opportunity created

4. Welcome email sent

5. Follow-up sequence begins

6. Lead books discovery call

7. Opportunity moves to Discovery Call Booked stage

8. Confirmation email sent

9. Calendar invitation sent

10. Internal notification sent to business owner

11. Appointment reminders sent

12. Discovery call occurs

13. Proposal sent

14. Follow-up sequence begins

15. Opportunity won or lost

16. Long-term nurture begins if necessary
